acoustic research

July 17, 2021 One-minute read

Open Lab for Bio-Acoustics is a space dedicated to ecosystem acoustic research and monitoring. It’s aim is to cross disciplines and knowledge between field recordists, hackers, scientists, engineers, taxonomists, environmental activists passionate of living beings and natural systems. Through exchange we hope to create relationships, networks for creativity, to raise awareness on the current ecosystem failure and the decline of species diversity.
